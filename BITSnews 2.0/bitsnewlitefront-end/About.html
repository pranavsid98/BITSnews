ABOUT US

We’ve all envisioned it since the inception, but it wasn’t there. Something was needed, something that has it all. Be it for who are here or for who aren’t! A ‘tell-it-all’ portal! Multiple attempts have been made in the past to get it on, but all in vain. Something was done wrong. Idea is one, implementations are many! Focus is one, minds are many!

A Surreal Endeavour to glorify BITS Pilani!

From the time we decided on coming to BITS Pilani and googling everything about it, trying to find a compiled a portsl got all our needs but it just wasn't there and to the time when we've graduated and want to know about what-how-why of our Alma Mater, for all that, we bring you BITSNewsLite!

For all on-campus activities! For all BITSian ventures and endeavours! A news hub of all BITSian activities! And a beacon of BITSian awesomeness for the world!

In concordance with clubs and departments of BITS Pilani, we’re presenting you BITS NewsLite which is a platform for BITSian community to showcase their creative, literary and technical talent. We will be putting up articles of all genre, interviews, polls, event updates and general news.
