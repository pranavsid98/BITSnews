The macbook pro is not as good as you think

October 31,2016 BitsNewsLite

Trivedi’s music has a refined sense of instrumentation and
follows a more varied range of orchestrated music.
He often experiments with his music style, changing
aspects of the musical tune simultaneously in order to establish a
different phase of soundtrack genre.

Simple, evocative tunes with a gradual build-up and a rousing ending,
the oh- so- gentle melodies, mesmerising, almost hypnotic backgrounds,
the beauty with which the songs play just enough to let the movie do the magic-
you know it’s Amit Trivedi when the music plays.
To stop the flow of his music would be like stopping of time itself,
incredible and inconceivable.
In his younger days, an electronic musical instrument fascinated Trivedi
and he was slowly drawn towards music, mainly influenced by classical music.
He went on to compose for theatre, background music for television shows,
live performances etc. Film music eventually came his way,
when friend and playback singer Shilpa Rao suggested his name to film director Anurag Kashyap.
After meeting Trivedi, Kashyap offered him the chance to compose the soundtrack to Dev.D.
Dev.D’s music got critical acclamation. He was conferred with both the R. D.
Burman Award and the Best Background Score at the
Filmfare Awards 2010, before receiving the National Film Award
for Best Music Direction, the highest recognition
for Indian composers.

With a very peculiar way of composition and use of musical
instruments, especially the fusion of rock music with regional instruments,
to create well blended fusion music without exploiting its originality is
noteworthy. No one uses folk singers better than him.
He never received any formal training in singing. He sings what
sounds good to him and what follows is music of mystical resonance which makes
people cry, laugh and feel their hair stand up.

This OASIS, join us in this musical extravaganza
with Coke Studio combining myriad musical influences
and be prepared to let the synchronised melodies consume you.
