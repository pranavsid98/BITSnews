ABOUT US


OUR STORY
We have all envisioned it since the inception of BITS- a tell it all portal. Multiple attempts have been made in the past to get it on, but all in vain. We wanted to create something that stands out in a crowd.
When we first thought about making an online BITSian newspaper , we were like… Is it possible? But then again through hard work, enthusiasm, dedication and belief ….. WE DID IT. Its not about you or me. Its about BITS Pilani.

WHO ARE WE?
A group of enthusiastic developers from BITS FIREFOX COMMUNITY.
We are affiliated to Mozilla.

OUR VISION
We plan to develop this site into a full fledged hub for all BITSian activities and ventures.
In concordance with clubs and departments of BITS Pilani, we’re presenting you a platform for BITSian community to showcase their creative, literary and technical talent.
We will be putting up general news stories, articles of all genres, interviews, polls and event updates.
Just imagine 10 years from now when all of you would be busy with your hectic schedules. Then also you would have a medium to get news of every new event happening in your alma mater.
Thats what we desire. Period.
Guest articles and opinions are always welcome. Drop us a mail below.
